FAQ | ClipArchive

What is Clipboard?

Clipboard is a productivity tool that allows you to track and organize everything you copy. It securely stores your snippets in the cloud, enabling you to access them instantly on all your devices.

How does Clipboard sync across devices?

Clipboard utilizes end-to-end encryption to securely sync your snippets across all your devices. This ensures that your data remains private and accessible only to you.

Can I access my Clipboard history offline?

Yes, Clipboard provides offline access to your history. Any changes or additions made while offline will be synced across your devices once you regain an internet connection.

Is Clipboard compatible with different operating systems?

Absolutely! Clipboard is available for iOS, macOS, Windows, Android, and Linux, ensuring seamless integration across all major operating systems.

How can I organize my Clipboard snippets?

Clipboard offers various organizational features, including categorization by content, category, web address, application, and more. You can also create custom tags and folders to further organize your snippets.

Does Clipboard support collaboration features?

Yes, Clipboard offers collaboration features, allowing you to share snippets with colleagues or collaborators. You can also set permissions to control who can view or edit shared snippets.

How secure is Clipboard's encryption?

Clipboard employs state-of-the-art encryption protocols to safeguard your data. Our encryption standards ensure that your snippets remain secure and protected from unauthorized access.

Can I customize the appearance of Clipboard?

Yes, Clipboard provides customization options to tailor the appearance and behavior of the app to your preferences. You can adjust themes, font sizes, and layout settings to create a personalized experience.

Does Clipboard offer integration with other apps?

Absolutely! Clipboard seamlessly integrates with popular productivity apps such as Microsoft Office, Google Workspace, and Slack. This allows you to easily transfer snippets between Clipboard and your favorite tools.

Is there a limit to the number of snippets I can store?

Clipboard offers generous storage capacity for your snippets, with no strict limit on the number of items you can store. However, we recommend periodically organizing and archiving old snippets to maintain optimal performance.
